Redesign

Packaging

For this project, we were tasked to redesign a brand and its packaging with a partner. We chose Astro Yogurt due to its traditional packaging. Consumers today prefer clean, easy-to-navigate packaging that highlights key information. A simplified design makes Astro more approachable, easier to identify on shelves, and instantly recognizable as a high-quality Canadian product.
